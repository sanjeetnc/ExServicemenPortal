About Us

Dedicated to the welfare of Ex-Servicemen, supporting farmers and strengthening communities.

Ex-Servicemen Welfare & Jai Jawan Jai Kisan Trust

The Ex-Servicemen Welfare & Jai Jawan Jai Kisan Trust is committed to serving veterans, supporting farmers, and creating sustainable community development initiatives.

Through welfare schemes, healthcare support, pension assistance, educational initiatives, farmer development programs and trust activities, we strive to improve lives and build stronger communities.

Mission

To provide welfare support, guidance, opportunities and assistance to veterans, farmers and their families.

Vision

To create empowered communities where veterans and farmers receive the support, recognition and opportunities they deserve.
