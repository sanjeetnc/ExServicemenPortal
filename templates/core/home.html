About Us

Dedicated To Supporting Our Nation's Veterans

The Ex-Servicemen Welfare & Jai Jawan Jai Kisan Trust serves as a comprehensive platform dedicated to the welfare of veterans, farmers and rural communities. The portal provides access to welfare schemes, pension services, healthcare benefits, educational assistance, employment opportunities and official circulars

Inspired by the spirit of "Jai Jawan Jai Kisan", the organisation works to honour those who served the nation while empowering communities through social development, awareness programmes and welfare initiatives.
